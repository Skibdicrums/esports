Twisted History

Founded in 2021 in Saudi Arabia by Ibrahim Bin Jibreen (CEO) and Hatem Almukhailed (Co-founder), Twisted Minds began as a PUBG Mobile friends team that entered a local tournament and then adopted the Twisted Minds name.

In 2022 the team became an official organization and expanded into Valorant, Overwatch, and Rocket League. In 2023 they exploded onto the world stage with Gamers8 and global recognition.

By 2024 Twisted Minds had grown to 23 teams across 19 games, and in 2025 they expanded into Asia, China, and the Philippines while pushing hard in world championships.
