Team Falcons, formerly known as Riyadh Falcons, is a powerhouse in the esports industry, representing Saudi Arabia with pride. Founded with a vision to dominate global competitions, the team has rapidly risen through the ranks.

In 2025, Falcons achieved unprecedented success, securing victories in key tournaments and establishing themselves as a force to be reckoned with. As we approach EWC 2026, the team continues to innovate and build on its legacy.
